Tech to look for: MusicCam

Meet MusicCam, the world’s first camera headset that sees and hears like you do. A camera, recorder, and headphones for music all in one headset!

Forget heavy storage. MusicCam keeps it light with a built-in 32Gb buffer — just enough to hold your shots while you move. Capture, transfer instantly via Wi-Fi, and keep recording without missing a beat.

Shaky roads? Crashing waves? MusicCam keeps it all smooth. With advanced 6-axis stabilization, every frame stays sharp and cinematic—no gimbals, no mounts, just pure motion.

Words from the Creator, Ryan

We are VibeLens! After hundreds of hours designing, testing, and getting wet (a lot), we built something that feels invisible — but unforgettable. MusicCam lets you live fully and share freely. Because life doesn’t wait for perfect framing, and stories aren’t meant to be staged. They’re meant to be lived — and captured, effortlessly.
